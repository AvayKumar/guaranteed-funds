Concept

Guaranteed Funds is a peer to peer donation community where members can help one another financially. The fund provides a system where members can freely give and receive financial donations from one another. Guaranteed Funds is founded by a team of financial specialists who have chosen to establish financial freedom in developing countries by creating a community where money circulates freely. It is a community where members assist one another financially by providing and receiving financial help to one another thus empowering all. This will also establish and grow businesses and also create financial freedom.

HOW IT WORKS

After signing up with Guaranteed Funds community, you will donate the sum of either ₦10,000, ₦30,000, ₦100,000, ₦200,000 or ₦500,000 to a fellow participant assigned by an automated system. After this member confirms receipt of your donation, the system will automatically assign two other participants to pay you the amount invested, thus giving you 100% of your investment (i.e. 100% of ₦10,000 is ₦20,000; 100% of ₦30,000 is ₦60,000, etc). Return of investment is within 48 hours to 14 working days after confirmation of receipt of donation. 

Referral bonus is paired to get help every 14days. The more you refer, the higher your referral bonus. 

All donations are made directly to participants. Guaranteed Funds is not liable for any losses made and will not collect any payment from participants as we do not run a central account. This is a community of mutual financial help.

Guaranteed Funds thrives with the influx of new members in the community. So we encourage members to use their referral links to earn more money and also invite friends and family to the Guaranteed Funds community so they can also gain financial freedom.

Members get help between 48hours and 14 working days. This time frame is dependent on the influx of new members into the community.

STEP-BY-STEP

Step 1:

After registration, click on the PLANS button on your dashboard and select the desired package and wait to be matched with another member of the community who is eligible to get help.

Step 2:

When you provide help, you are required to upload a proof of payment, otherwise you will be blocked if you do not. Make sure the other participant is reachable before you provide help and write to support if the other participant bluntly refuses to confirm the donation after receiving it.

Step 3:

After providing help to another participant you will be automatically paired to get help within 48hours to 14 days after confirmation of your payment. If the participant matched to you fails to pay within 24hrs, the system will remove and block the participant and automatically match another participant to pay you.

It is advisable to use ATM transfers, or mobile and internet banking on weekends. Users who do not use such methods of payment are advised to request to provide help only on week-days using bank deposit Guaranteed Funds is community of mutual financial help and does not collect money from its members. Members freely help each other by donating a specified amount of their choice. This is not illegal as giving a fellow participant financial assistance is not prohibited by international or local constitutions or legal systems
